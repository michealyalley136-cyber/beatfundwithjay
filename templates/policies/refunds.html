Refund & Cancellation Policy

This policy explains how refunds and cancellations work across marketplace purchases and service bookings. BeatFund is not a bank. When enabled, card payments are processed by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe).

Marketplace (digital goods)

  • Refund eligibility depends on licensing terms and whether deliverables have been provided/downloaded.
  • Digital goods may be non-refundable once delivered unless required by law or due to verified fraud.

Bookings (services)

  • Cancellation/refund eligibility depends on booking status, timing, and evidence of non-delivery.
  • Providers and clients should attempt resolution in-chat before escalating.

Hold fee mechanics

  • Some bookings may require a hold fee that is held until completion or dispute resolution.
  • Hold fees may be released, refunded, or forfeited depending on outcome and policy rules.

Fees

  • Platform fees and processor fees (if any) may be non-refundable once a transaction completes.
  • Any fees will be disclosed before checkout.

Chargebacks and account restrictions

  • Chargebacks may lead to temporary holds, refunds, and account restrictions.
  • Accounts may be limited to comply with fraud prevention and legal obligations.
