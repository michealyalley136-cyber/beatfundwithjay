Risk Disclosure (VERY IMPORTANT)

Using BeatFund involves risk. Please read this carefully before using the wallet, marketplace, or booking features.

  • Funding a creator does not guarantee results or returns
  • Projects may fail or be delayed
  • BeatFund is not a bank and does not insure wallet balances
  • Wallet balances are not FDIC insured and are not bank deposits
  • Users may lose money

Marketplace (digital goods)

  • Digital goods (beats, licenses, deliverables) may have licensing limitations and usage restrictions.
  • Once a deliverable is provided, refunds may be limited based on the Refund & Cancellation Policy.
  • Exclusive items can become unavailable after purchase.

Bookings (services)

  • BeatFund does not guarantee service delivery, outcomes, or satisfaction between clients and providers.
  • Disputes may require evidence and can result in holds, refunds, or account restrictions.

Hold fees (escrow-like mechanics)

  • Some bookings may require a hold fee that is held until booking completion or dispute resolution.
  • Hold fees may be released, refunded, or forfeited depending on outcomes and policy rules.

Payments, chargebacks, and restrictions

  • When enabled, card payments are processed by third-party processors (e.g., Stripe). BeatFund does not store full card numbers.
  • Chargebacks and disputes can result in temporary holds, refunds, and account restrictions.
  • Accounts may be limited or frozen to comply with fraud prevention and legal obligations.

You should only use funds you can afford to lose.
