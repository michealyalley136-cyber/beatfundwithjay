Dispute & Chargeback Policy

This policy describes how disputes are handled for marketplace purchases and service bookings. BeatFund is not a bank. When enabled, payments are processed by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe).

General process

  1. Parties should attempt resolution directly using BeatFund chat and platform tools.
  2. If unresolved, BeatFund may request evidence (messages, delivery details, timestamps, files).
  3. BeatFund may apply temporary holds or restrictions while investigating.
  4. Decisions are made to protect platform integrity and reduce fraud/abuse.

Service delivery disputes (bookings)

  • Disputes may consider booking status, communications, and any proof of delivery/non-delivery.
  • Hold fees (if applicable) may be released, refunded, or forfeited depending on outcome.

Digital goods disputes (marketplace)

  • Disputes may consider licensing terms and whether deliverables were provided/downloaded.
  • Refund outcomes can be limited once a deliverable is provided, except where required by law or for verified fraud.

Chargebacks

  • Chargebacks can result in temporary holds, refunds, and account restrictions.
  • Repeated chargebacks or suspected fraud may lead to suspension or permanent removal.
