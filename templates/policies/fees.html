Fees & Pricing Policy

BeatFund charges transparent platform service fees on transactions. All fees are clearly displayed before checkout.

Beat Marketplace Fees

When purchasing beats from the marketplace:

  • Platform service fee: 12% of the beat price
  • Minimum fee: $1.29 per purchase
  • Maximum fee: $5.17 per purchase

BookMe Service Booking Fees

When booking services (studios, videographers, talent, etc.):

  • Platform service fee: 8% of the booking subtotal
  • Minimum fee: $3.99 per booking
  • Maximum fee: $49.99 per booking

Payment Processing Fees

Payment processing fees (Stripe) are displayed for transparency on all purchase confirmations. Currently, Stripe payment processing is not enabled, so these fees are shown as $0.00. When Stripe is enabled, actual processing fees will be calculated and displayed based on Stripe's fee structure.

Transparency

All fees are clearly shown before checkout:

  • Subtotal (item/service price)
  • BeatFund service fee
  • Payment processing (Stripe) fee
  • Total due today

BeatFund does not charge hidden fees. You will always see the complete breakdown before confirming any purchase.
