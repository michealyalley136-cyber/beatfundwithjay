Wallet Disclosure

BeatFund is a software platform that provides tools for creator marketplaces, bookings, and payment workflows. BeatFund is not a bank.

Payments & payouts

  • Payments are processed by third-party providers (e.g., Stripe) when enabled.
  • BeatFund does not store full card numbers or full bank account details.
  • Wallet balances are platform balances for using BeatFund features.

No FDIC insurance

Unless explicitly stated otherwise for a specific payment product and program, wallet balances are not FDIC insured and are not deposits in a bank account.

Use within BeatFund

Keep communication and payments inside BeatFund. Do not share contact or payment information to move transactions off-platform. This helps prevent fraud and supports dispute handling.

Disputes & refunds

See our dispute and refund policies for how chargebacks, refunds, and booking disputes are handled.
